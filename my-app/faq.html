Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about posting jobs and finding tradespeople.

🔹 For Clients — Posting & Managing Jobs

How to find great tradespeople and get things done.

How do I post a job on TradeConnect?

Create a free account, tap Post a Job, and fill in the details — what you need, your location, and a preferred timeframe. You can add photos to help tradespeople understand the work. Your job goes live within minutes.

Is it free to post a job?

Yes — posting a job is completely free for clients. There are no sign-up fees or listing charges. You only pay once you've accepted a quote and the job is complete.

How quickly will I hear from tradespeople?

Most jobs get their first response within a few hours. Jobs marked as Urgent are shown at the top of the supplier feed and tend to get replies even faster. You'll get a notification as soon as someone shows interest.

Can I choose which tradesperson does my job?

Absolutely — you're always in control. Browse profiles, read verified reviews, and compare quotes before you decide. Nobody is ever assigned to your job automatically.

What types of jobs can I post?

TradeConnect covers a huge range of services — plumbing, electrical, carpentry, plastering, painting, roofing, gardening, cleaning, removals, and much more. If it's a home or trade task, there's likely a supplier for it.

How does payment work?

When you accept a quote, your payment is held securely in escrow. It's only released to the tradesperson once you confirm the job is done — so you're fully protected. We accept all major cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

Don't mark the job as complete — raise a dispute through the Dispute Centre in the app within 72 hours. Your payment stays held in escrow while we investigate. Our team resolves disputes within 5 business days.

🔨 For Tradespeople — Finding & Completing Work

How to find jobs and grow your business on TradeConnect.

How do I sign up as a tradesperson?

Download the app or visit our website, tap Join as a Supplier, and complete your profile — your trade, service area, a short bio, and any certifications or insurance. The whole process takes around 10 minutes.

How do I find and accept jobs?

Your job feed automatically shows open jobs that match your trade and location. Tap a job to see the full details, then express interest or send a quote. When a client accepts, you're connected via in-app chat to arrange the details.

Is there a cost to join as a tradesperson?

We offer a free basic plan with a limited number of quotes per month — great for getting started. Our Pro plan gives you unlimited quotes and priority placement in job feeds. See our Pricing page for current rates.

Can I set my own rates?

Yes, completely. TradeConnect never sets your prices. You can submit a fixed-price quote, an hourly rate, or request a site visit before committing to a number. Your business, your prices.

How do I set my service area?

Go to Settings → Service Area and set a radius from your base postcode (e.g. 15 miles), or list specific towns. You'll only see — and appear in — jobs within your chosen area. You can update this at any time.

How do I get a Verified badge?

Upload your trade qualifications, public liability insurance, and a photo ID. Our team reviews it within 2 business days. Verified suppliers get a badge, rank higher in job feeds, and win more client trust.

When and how do I get paid?

Payment is held in secure escrow when a client accepts your quote. Once they confirm the job is done, funds are released to your bank account within 1–3 business days. Track it all from your Earnings dashboard.

Still have a question?

Our support team is available 7 days a week, 8am – 8pm.
